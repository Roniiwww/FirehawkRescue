Firehawk Drone

Revolutionary life-saving technology designed by an 8th grade student to make emergency response faster, safer, and more effective.

Mission Statement

To create an autonomous drone system that can rapidly respond to emergency situations, providing critical support in search and rescue operations, medical supply delivery, and disaster relief efforts.

About Firehawk

Advanced drone technology designed to save lives through innovative engineering and cutting-edge automation.

Search & Rescue

Equipped with thermal imaging and GPS tracking to locate missing persons in challenging terrain and weather conditions.

Medical Supply Delivery

Rapid deployment of essential medical supplies to remote or inaccessible areas during emergencies.

Disaster Response

Real-time monitoring and assessment of disaster zones, providing critical data to first responders.
